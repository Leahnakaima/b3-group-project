Innovation

Innovation is the process of creating value by applying novel solutions to meaningful problems. Infrastructure

Infrastructure are the basic physical systems of businesses or nations. They include transportation, communication, sewage, water, and electric systems. Two infrastructure that are necessary for digital platforms are explored here and they include:(i) Secure Internet servers (per 1 million people). (ii) International Internet bandwidth (bit/s) per internet user.

Demand

Demand is the consumers' desire to purchase goods and services, and their willingness to pay for them. This website explores two components of demand that are valuable for digital platforms and they include: (i) Disporsable income. (ii) Consumer expenditure.

Governance

Governance is how nations, societies, or groups within them organize to make decisions. This website explores two components of governance that are valuable to digital platforms and they include: (i) E-governance Development Index. (ii) Intellectual property protection.
